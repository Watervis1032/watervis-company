1,032m Deep Sea Water

1,032m Below the Surface,
Where WATERVIS Begins

WATERVIS sources deep sea water from the deep offshore waters of Yangyang. The source water is desalinated, and its mineral concentration and hardness are adjusted for use in products and ingredients.

ORIGINDeep-Water Intake

Source water from 1,032 meters in the East Sea is transported to the onshore plant.

PROCESSMineral Adjustment

Desalination, concentration, and blending are used to create water suited to each application.

APPLICATIONMultiple Applications

Developed into drinking water, ingredients, salt, and OEM/ODM products.

Technology & Production

From Intake to Finished Product
An Integrated Production System

The complex manufacturing process is presented in five core stages. Details can be updated as operations and equipment evolve.

STEP 01

Intake

Transport deep sea water from offshore Yangyang to the onshore plant.

STEP 02

Desalination

Separate salt from water through RO processing to produce treated water.

STEP 03

Mineral Adjustment

Blend concentrated water and desalinated water to achieve the target hardness.

STEP 04

Product Manufacturing

Produce drinking deep sea water and a range of applied products.

STEP 05

Packaging & Shipping

Package finished products, verify quality, and ship.
